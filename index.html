How To Play
Colordle is a spinoff of Wordle designed around the RGB color system. Learn more about RGB here.
The goal of colordle is to guess the RGB value of the given color within a distance of 20, calculated by the Euclidean distance. The smaller the distance, the closer your guess is. You have unlimited attempts.
For each attempt use the sliders or input numbers for the RGB value. The maximum input for each is 255. Hit submit to enter your guess.
After you hit enter, five colored balls will appear, as shown below.

The first three balls represent the RGB values entered as an attempt. The numbers represent the values. The color of the balls represent how close your guess is. View the gradient below:
The fourth ball's color indicates the color of your guess. The number indicates the Euclidean distance between your color and the correct color.
The last ball's color indicates the correct color. The number indicates which attempt you are on.
